Deaf Community Interpreter Project

Bridging communication gaps for the Deaf in South Africa.

Key Statistics on Deafness in South Africa

Approximately 4 million South Africans have significant hearing loss. Major barriers in healthcare, education, and employment affect accessibility for Deaf individuals.

Education Challenges

70% of South African Deaf learners do not finish high school due to limited access to SASL-based education.

Employment Obstacles

Many employers lack tools to facilitate communication in SASL, contributing to high unemployment rates among the Deaf.

Healthcare Communication Barriers

Deaf patients face risks of miscommunication, especially in emergencies where SASL interpreters are unavailable.

Project Mission

By using AI-driven interpretation, this project aims to improve accessibility in key sectors, reducing reliance on human interpreters and offering scalable solutions.
