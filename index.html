Breaking Down Barriers: A New Era of Inclusive Communication

Empowering Deaf Individuals with Real-Time Sign Language Interpretation

"Imagine walking into a healthcare facility, in urgent need of care, only to face silence. No explanations, no reassurance—just confusion. Deaf South Africans endure this daily. But we're here to change that."

Key Statistics: Deaf Individuals in Healthcare

  • Communication Barriers: Deaf South Africans frequently experience difficulties communicating with healthcare professionals. Without South African Sign Language (SASL) interpreters, critical health information is often misunderstood or missed entirely, leading to inadequate care.
  • Lack of Interpreter Availability: Many hospitals and clinics lack on-site interpreters, especially in emergency situations. As a result, Deaf patients may rely on written notes or family members, which can compromise their privacy and understanding.
  • High Risks in Medical Situations: Miscommunication during medical consultations can lead to incorrect diagnoses, mistreatment, or failure to follow post-care instructions, endangering the lives of Deaf patients.
  • Limited Accessibility Resources: Few healthcare facilities offer accessible communication tools, leaving Deaf patients at a significant disadvantage when navigating hospital settings or accessing emergency services.

Our Solution

Our innovative program interprets gestures in real-time and plays them back in any South African language, allowing Deaf users to express themselves freely. It also lets hearing users communicate seamlessly by speaking in any South African language, with their words displayed as text on the screen for Deaf users. By bridging this communication gap, we make healthcare, education, and workplaces more inclusive.

Key Benefits

  • Accessibility and Equity: By making healthcare settings deaf-friendly, we align with global goals for equality, giving Deaf South Africans a dignified healthcare experience.
  • Reducing Interpreter Reliance: Our 24/7, on-demand technology eliminates the need for physical interpreters, saving costs and offering reliable communication at all times.
  • Scalability for All Settings: Our solution integrates seamlessly across industries, from healthcare to retail to education, offering an accessible and flexible communication tool wherever it’s needed.

Expanded Use Cases

Our technology goes beyond healthcare, reaching into other areas where Deaf individuals face barriers:

  • Education: Real-time interpretation can support Deaf students in classrooms, reducing educational disparities.
  • Corporate Offices: Empowering companies to achieve diversity and inclusion goals, creating accessible communication for Deaf employees.
  • Customer Service: Enabling large retailers to communicate effectively with Deaf customers, enhancing their shopping experience.
  • Transportation Hubs: Facilitating precise communication for Deaf travelers in airports and bus stations.
